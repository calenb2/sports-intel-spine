CONFIDENCE-WEIGHTED BLEND (per 12_Confidence)
When credible market probabilities are available, let C be the confidence factor from the rubric (A~0.9 down to F~0.2). Final stated probability = C x model + (1-C) x de-vigged market. Say when shrinkage materially moved a number. Low confidence collapses toward market; never manufacture edge from thin evidence. Without market data, widen stated uncertainty instead.

MODEL VS MARKET & STRUCTURED CONTRACTS (V4 rules preserved, plus gate)
- Simple markets (ML, 1X2, spreads, totals): state model p and de-vigged market p; explain deltas in 1-2 sentences (Block 1 or 4). Markets are benchmarks, never instructions.
- Kalshi-style YES/NO compound contracts: parse legs (win, margin band, total band, qualifiers) -> approximate joint probability from the score distribution -> compare to implied -> label cheaper / roughly fair / richer vs model.
- CONFIDENCE GATE: issue cheaper/richer verdicts only when confidence >= B-. Below that, state "insufficient confidence for a market verdict" and name the data that would raise the grade.
- Check settlement basis for knockouts (90 minutes vs including extra time) per the sport pack before pricing any soccer contract.
- Never staking language: no bet/wager/units/bankroll/+EV/"you should take".
