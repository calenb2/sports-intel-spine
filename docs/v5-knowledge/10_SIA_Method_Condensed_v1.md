# 10 — SIA Method Condensed (v1)

Operational digest of the Sports Intelligence Analysis Method (full doctrine: scholarly document + workbook, outside the GPT). North star: statistics say what is likely; sports science says whether the body can support it; tactics say why it happened; psychology says whether they execute under pressure — and governance says how much to trust the answer.

## 1. Analysis modes (never conflate)
Descriptive (what happened) · Diagnostic (why) · Predictive (what next) · Prescriptive (what to change) · Counterfactual (what if) · Developmental (how ability is changing). Predictions are Predictive mode; POST_GAME_REVIEW is Descriptive+Diagnostic; describing the past never auto-counts as predicting.

## 2. The fifteen sections — checklists
Each: core question → key outputs → confidence penalties.

**SIA-01 Ontology & Context** — What exactly is evaluated, at what level, over what horizon? → unit/phase/role map (sport packs). Penalty: ambiguous rules/format.
**SIA-02 Measurement Validity & Provenance** — Can the evidence be trusted? → source grades A–F, vintage echo, missing-data flags. Penalty: unverified vendor data, vague definitions, unmatched timestamps.
**SIA-03 Statistical Modeling** — What is likely, and how wide is uncertainty? → win prob, ranges, calibration. Penalty: small samples, drift, uncalibrated outputs.
**SIA-04 Tactical Analysis** — What mechanism produced performance? → matchup map, phase edges, game-state behavior. Penalty: no video/tag support, confounded game states.
**SIA-05 Causal & Counterfactual** — What actually changed outcomes? → labeled causal claims, what-if scenarios with assumptions. Penalty: correlation-as-cause, no comparison group.
**SIA-06 Skill & Development** — Is change durable or situational? → trend vs blip classification. Penalty: sparse history, role changes.
**SIA-07 Physical Capacity** — Can the body produce the required output? → role-fit, decay forecast. Penalty: outdated tests, environment mismatch.
**SIA-08 Load & Recovery** — Adapting, fatigued, or at risk? → readiness/fatigue flags. Penalty: missing internal load, schedule anomalies.
**SIA-09 Injury & Availability** — Who plays, how much, how well? → availability probability × expected workload × limitation. Penalty: vague reports, hidden minutes caps. Clearance ≠ fit ≠ sharp ≠ durable.
**SIA-10 Nutrition & Weight** — Does fueling support demands? (weight-class/endurance/heat contexts). Penalty: self-report only.
**SIA-11 Psychological Performance** — Execute under pressure? → pressure-situation record, decision stability. Penalty: small pressure samples, narrative bias. Observable behavior beats adjectives.
**SIA-12 Welfare & Ethics** — Any boundary that shapes use? → allowed-use rules (see 12_Confidence §6).
**SIA-13 Environment & Rules** — What external conditions shift value or variance? → context adjustment + variance flag. Penalty: unknown weather/surface/officials.
**SIA-14 Adversarial & Game Theory** — How do opponents adapt, hide, or exploit? → red-team note, deception/incentive check. Penalty: unverified rumor, hidden incentives. Opponents are not static.
**SIA-15 Decision Translation & Governance** — How should this be used and how do we learn? → confidence grade, tripwires, postmortem row, update trigger. Penalty: no backtest, no owner, no log.

## 3. Evidence vs confidence
Evidence score = how strongly signals point one way. Confidence = how trustworthy the evidence is. Report both implicitly: probability (evidence) + grade (confidence). Never average them into one number.

## 4. Decision brief fields (for recommendation-style asks)
Conclusion (prob/range/tier) · Top drivers (with section IDs) · Risks & tripwires · Missing data & penalties · Recommended action with owner, timing, monitor metric, update trigger · Post-event review plan.

## 5. Failure modes to self-check before sending
Dashboard worship (SIA-02) · expensive horoscope overfit (SIA-03) · film-room theory that dies on the scoreboard (SIA-04) · hot-month-as-skill (SIA-06) · "available"-as-one-field (SIA-09) · locker-room mythology (SIA-11) · perfect baseline in imaginary physics (SIA-13) · static-NPC opponents (SIA-14) · brilliant insight with no owner or log (SIA-15).
